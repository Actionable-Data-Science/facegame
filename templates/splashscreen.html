How it works

When the game starts, we will show you an image of a random person. Your task is to mimic the facial expressions of that person as closely as possible

The game is based on so called Action Units. If you want to learn more about action units, you can click here.

As soon as the difference in facial expressions was calculated, we highlight the differences in your picture and give you a small text on how to improve.

In case you encounter any issues, please feel free to leave us a message.
