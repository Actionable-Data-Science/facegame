Welcome to FaceGame!

Are you ready to put your facial expression skills to the test? Get ready for a hilarious and exciting game where you'll be recreating facial expressions like a pro!

How to Play:

  1. Look at the image of a person's face showing a specific expression.
  2. Get ready to make that same expression with your own face!
  3. Strike a pose and let your facial muscles do the talking.
  4. Submit your expression and let our AI system do the rest!

Our super-smart AI will analyze your expression and give you a score. Aim for perfection to earn those high scores and show off your expressive prowess!

Why Play Anonymous or Sign Up:

We understand that privacy matters. That's why we give you the option to play anonymously. No images of you will be collected, just anonymized information like your score. But if you want to take it to the next level and be part of something big, sign up and donate your data. You'll be contributing to groundbreaking research in facial recognition technology!

By donating your data, you become a superhero helping us train our AI models to detect facial action units with greater accuracy. Action units are like the secret language of facial expressions! Imagine how awesome it would be to be part of scientific progress while having a blast playing this game!

Plus, as a registered user, you'll have the power to delete your data at any time, request a copy of it, and be treated like a VIP. We value and respect your trust!

So, are you ready to unlock your facial expression genius and embark on this thrilling adventure? Play anonymously or sign up today and let's make facial recognition history together!
